Investment Philosophy

I don’t try to predict—I prepare. I translate macro signals into clear rules, allocate assets by purpose, and rebalance with discipline. By combining theory and practice, focusing on asymmetric risk–reward, and letting compounding work over time, I pursue stable growth and long-term value creation.

Risk First

Protect downside via position sizing, options overlays, and scenario testing before seeking returns.

Macro + Quant

Blend macro judgment with quantitative signals across FX derivatives and real assets.

Purposeful Allocation

Assign assets to roles—growth, income, hedge—and rebalance on a set cadence.
